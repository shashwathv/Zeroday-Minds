About InboxArmor

InboxArmor is a smart browser extension that uses the power of Google Gemini AI to detect phishing attempts inside your emails in real-time. Our tool empowers you with instant awareness, helping you stay protected from malicious links and deceptive content — all without compromising privacy.

Key Features

Real-Time Scanning

Analyzes your opened emails instantly and flags suspicious ones.

AI Powered

Utilizes cutting-edge LLM (Gemini) for deep phishing detection.

Lightweight & Fast

Minimal memory usage, blazing fast response.

Tech Stack

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Particles.js, Google Gemini API, Manifest V3
Built for Chrome & Firefox.
